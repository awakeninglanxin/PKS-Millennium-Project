I don’t wanna get philosophical, but I think it’s imperative to keep in mind during any of this, what something is or isn’t, comes down to two aspects: the information and the interpreter. If two things interpret that information in different ways; that information is two things.

What’s inside the prime sequence, is complex. Not really regarding the concepts, but complex with regards to layers and hidden forms. I have been thinking about the translation of structures and patterns from 0d to 2d a lot throughout this endeavor; it made sense to me all these structures are easily transferable from those dimensions. But I was shocked when I found clear structures, that really can only be found inside the 3rd dimension, encoded in this complex system. The layers of overlapping patterns from different resolutions/frequencies happening at once, continuously scaling to different sizes, is why this system has been so confusing.

I don’t know how many layers of patterns there are in this system. Or in how many dimensions. We may be finding them for a long time. I hope others get involved.
